1. Give (Even Small Amounts Help)

  • Christian Mission to Gaza (CM2G) — Food, water, shelter from Christian families in Gaza.
  • Palestine Children’s Relief Fund (PCRF) — Medical aid, food, trauma care.
  • Gaza Survival Crowdfunding (via Pleasure Pie) — Direct mutual aid to Gazan educators and families.

2. Join Direct Action / Advocacy Groups

  • Jewish Voice for Peace
  • IfNotNow
  • US Campaign for Palestinian Rights
  • International Solidarity Movement
  • Boycott, Divest, Sanctions (BDS) Movement

3. Buy and Share Solidarity Materials

  • Palestine map bracelet (from Wear the Peace) — Donates meals.
  • Solidarity stickers and keffiyeh gear on Etsy
  • Free Palestine zines (Pleasure Pie)

4. Weekly Action Plan

  • Mon – Donate $5–50 to a charity
  • Tues – Read a Palestinian story or watch a short doc
  • Wed – Wear a bracelet or keffiyeh + carry a zine to give away
  • Thurs – Contact a representative to demand ceasefire / stop arms
  • Fri – Share a post, quote, or zine from a Gazan voice
  • Sat – Go to a protest, vigil, or discussion
  • Sun – Rest, reflect, plan next week, and encourage one friend
